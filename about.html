Our Team

Dr. Nishit Gokarne

Dr. Nishit Gokarn has grown up around dogs as pets all his life. Dr. Sanjana Karve

Dr. Sanjana Karve has grown up with dogs rather than siblings. Her childhood vision was to become a veterinary surgeon.
